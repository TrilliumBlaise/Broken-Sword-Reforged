The thousand years of peace is now over.
The ancient foe, The Evil One, has arisen.
For the last several generations, it has been your families solemn duty to vanquish Him.
This is because the only weapon capable of defeating this monstrosity has been passed down through your generations.
This solemn duty has now passed to you.
It is your job to reforge the weapon to its full glory.
For this task, the ancient gods have placed crystals in the hearts of weapons forged through battle.
Seek the crystals!
Empower your weapon!
Defeat the Evil One!
Or the world
will never be the same...
